Changing your password

Once you’ve successfully logged in, you can change your password by clicking on the gear icon at the top right of the screen, selecting ‘Change Password’ and following the instructions:

You will be asked to enter your Old Password and your New Password. Ensure that the New Password meets the password requirements.

You will receive an email notification that your password has been successfully changed. If you receive this email but have not changed your password yourself than contact the inSilico support team at support@knoldus.com.
