For whom is this tool intended?

As our vision is to democratize advanced machine learning and AI, this tool is intended to initiate a seismic shift in who can leverage advanced modeling from exclusively large, resource rich teams to every team.  InSilico was created to enable anyone on a team—but specifically the subject matter expert (SME)—to single handedly execute the AI Development Life Cycle (ADLC).  By automating and abstracting the highly-technical and domain specific knowledge, we allow anyone to use this tool, from the neophyte just beginning to dabble in analytics to the advanced guru who wants to develop and execute the ADLC faster and more efficiently.
